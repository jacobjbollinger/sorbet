What Sorbet Is

Sorbet is a simple RSS feed fetcher/parser and sender, it takes and RSS feed and sends it to your inbox whenever there are updates.

What Sorbet Isn't

A "feed reader" in the usual sense, you can setup the feeds you want and check on their status but your email client is technically your feed reader now.

Why Sorbet

Most feed readers are built for quick browsing massive amounts of feeds. My email is built so that I am garenteed to see every email/post and read it. Why reinvent the wheel, just send them to something I already use.

Doesn't this service already exist?

Yes, and it isn't free or open source. I feel like such a simple service should be accessible and hostable anywhere. This app was built in a few hours and I see no reason to charge monthly feeds in the 5 dollar plus range for that. I may in the future ask for donations or require a small signup fee but if you sign up now you'll always have a free account with us!
