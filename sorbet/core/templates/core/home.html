Sorbet

A feed manager that sends feed updates directly to your inbox.

What Sorbet Is

Sorbet is a simple RSS feed fetcher/parser and sender, it takes and RSS feed and sends it to your inbox whenever it gets updated.

What Sorbet Isn't

A "feed reader" in the usual sense, you can setup the feeds you want and check on their status but your email client is technically your feed reader now.

Why Sorbet?

I've been all over the internets and have consumed enough data. I have a very small selection of feeds I wish to read on a regular basis and those feeds I never want to miss a single post on. Most feed readers are built for quick browsing massive amounts of feeds. My email is built so that I am garenteed to see every email/post and read it. Why reinvent the wheel, just sent them to something I already use.
