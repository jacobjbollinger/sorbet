What Sorbet Is.

Sorbet is a simple RSS feed fetcher/parser and sender. It takes an RSS feed and when there are updates it sends them to your inbox.

What Sorbet Isn't.

A traditional "feed reader". you can setup the feeds you want and check on their status, but your email client is your feed reader now.

Why Sorbet?

Most feed readers are built for quickly browsing massive amounts of feeds. My email is built so that I am guaranteed to see every email and read it.

Doesn't this service already exist?

Yes, and it isn't free or open source. I feel like such a simple service should be accessible and hostable anywhere. In the future I may ask for donations or require a small signup fee but if you sign up now you'll always have a free account and the software will always be open source.
